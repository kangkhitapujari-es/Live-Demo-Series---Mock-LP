How it works

Built for the teams running comp every day.

For Ops, Finance, Sales Comp, and RevOps practitioners who own the calc engine, not the executive review.

01 · Live workflows

One workflow per session, run end to end.

Each session bundles three or four modules around a problem comp teams work through every quarter. Close, mid-quarter plan changes via Time Machine, plan design with accelerators and SPIFFs, ICM migrations, and audit-ready reporting. The product runs the way it runs for paying customers.

02 · AE-hosted

Hosted by an AE who lives in this every day.

Everstage AEs spend their week with RevOps and Finance leaders running comp. They narrate the workflow the way a comp ops manager would, in the live product.

03 · 45-minute format

45 minutes. Calendar-friendly by design.

35 minutes of live workflow plus 10 minutes of Q&A. Registered questions go first, chat next. Show up, take one thing back to your team, get on with your day.
